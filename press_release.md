### New Open Dataset Published: Interactive dashboard: Neuroinflammation in Alzheimer's, Parkinson's and Multiple Sclerosis. DOI: 10.7910/DVN/X2TQQA

**Universidad Internacional de La Rioja (UNIR)** — Juan Moisés de la Serna Tuya (ORCID: https://orcid.org/0000-0002-8401-8018) has published a new open dataset: *Interactive dashboard: Neuroinflammation in Alzheimer's, Parkinson's and Multiple Sclerosis. DOI: 10.7910/DVN/X2TQQA*.
